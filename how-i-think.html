How I Think

This page outlines the principles and decision lenses that guide my work, particularly in situations where constraints are high and clarity is limited.

Design Principles

Business clarity before content volume. Learning effectiveness over visual polish. Accessibility and scalability are treated as baselines, not enhancements.

Decision-Making

I routinely navigate trade-offs between speed and depth, standardization and customization, and stakeholder urgency versus learning integrity.
